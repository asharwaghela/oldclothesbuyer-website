About Asha Old Clothes Buyer

Asha Old Clothes Buyer is a premier commercial circular textile platform dedicated to reducing carbon footprints and advancing circular textile economies. We operate as a full-service grading and logistics operator that facilitates doorstep collection, sanitization, sorting, and green distribution of unused or old garments, home fabrics, and footwears. Our infrastructure channels discarded fabrics back into active circles of use, raw material reclaiming, and commercial fiber recycling.

09

Accepted & Non-Accepted Items

To maintain safety, hygienic processing, and logistical standards, we apply strict acceptance criteria:

✅ Accepted Items

Wearable garments (shirts, pants, dresses), winters/woolen wear, bedsheets, blankets, curtains, towels, and matching pairs of shoes/footwears.

❌ Non-Accepted Items

Wet or damp clothing, moldy or infested items, fabrics contaminated with oils/chemicals, biological waste, single unmatched footwear, or torn rags.

10

Donation Service

When you opt for our "Donate" service, you voluntarily transfer ownership of your selected clothes to Asha Old Clothes Buyer free of monetary charge. Please note that donated items become part of our commercial collection, sorting, and processing pipelines. These items may be commercially sanitized, graded, and distributed through our commercial secondary reuse networks, processed into industrial wiping rags, processed into acoustic insulation, or handed over to our authorized partner organizations for secondary reuse and social welfare initiatives.

11

Sell Clothes Service

If you select our "Sell" service, you agree to sell your clothes to us at the evaluated spot rate. You understand and accept that our on-site clothing valuation is based strictly on the following parameters:

  • Condition: Physical wearability, level of fading, elasticity, or stretching of the items.
  • Quantity: Total weight of items measured in kilograms using our calibrated digital weighing scale.
  • Category: The ratio of premium high-value wearable items to recycling-grade waste.
  • Market Demand: Prevailing wholesale market value index for secondary garments and recycled raw cotton.
12

Pricing Policy

All pricing, scrap values, and purchasing rates are calculated based on our dynamic price charts, which are published and updated regularly on our Prices page. These prices reflect wholesale textile market indexes. Our on-site representative's physical assessment and weight calculation are final. We do not participate in secondary negotiations or manual bargaining during the doorstep collection process.

13

A digital receipt or confirmation will be issued via WhatsApp.

Booking Rejection Policy

Asha Old Clothes Buyer reserves the absolute right to reject any booking or cancel service on the spot. Common reasons for rejection include:

  • Pickup location not serviceable: Requested address falls outside active limits of Thane or Navi Mumbai.
  • Insufficient quantity: Total materials do not reach the minimum threshold weight (usually 20-30 kg).
  • Unsafe location: Environments posing environmental, safety, or health hazards to our field staff.
  • Incorrect information: False registration logs, non-functional phone numbers, or fraudulent address markers.
  • Restricted access: Gated societies or high-security complexes that deny entrance to our collection vehicles.
  • Other operational reasons: Unforeseen logistics disruptions, bad road access, or natural weather disruptions.

Security & Terms FAQs

Q1: Is Asha Old Clothes Buyer an NGO or charity?

No. We operate strictly as a private, commercial circular logistics and recycling venture. We do not issue tax-deductible receipts, 80G benefits, or charitable trust slips.

Q2: Are there doorstep collection charges?

No. Our doorstep collection and transit fleet services are completely free of charge in Thane and Navi Mumbai, provided you meet our minimum volume requirements.

Q3: What is the minimum clothing quantity for booking?

To cover transportation overheads and logistics costs, we request a minimum volume of 20 to 30 kg of clothes per doorstep pickup.

Q4: Can I choose whether to donate or sell my old garments?

Yes. Our platform offers a voluntary 'Donate' pipeline for circular reuse and a commercial 'Sell' pipeline where you receive spot evaluation values by weight.

Q5: How is the value of old clothes calculated?

Valuation is evaluated on-site by total weight in kilograms and graded based on fabric wearability, condition, and actual secondary textile market demand.

Q6: What clothes do you reject during pickup?

We strictly reject any wet or damp garments, moldy fabrics, clothing stained with oils or chemical paints, biological waste, or single unpaired footwears.

Q7: Can I reschedule my scheduled pickup time?

Yes. Rescheduling or cancellation is allowed free of charge at least 24 hours prior to your scheduled slot via our portal or WhatsApp help desk.

Q8: How is the payout made for sold clothes?

Our logistics representatives disburse payments instantly on the spot during weight collection using UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm), cash, or instant bank transfers.

Q9: What happens to donated clothes?

Donated garments are sorted, commercially sanitized, and distributed through circular second-hand pathways, processed into raw fibers, or channeled into eco-recycling networks.

Q10: Are you planning to serve cities other than Thane and Navi Mumbai?

Yes. We are pro-actively working to scale our circular logistics operations and hope to announce doorstep fleets in other parts of Maharashtra very soon.
