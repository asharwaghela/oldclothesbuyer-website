Terms & Conditions

Last Updated: 08 July 2026

Service Availability

Our doorstep collection and purchasing services are currently available only within municipal limits of Thane, Mumbai, and Navi Mumbai, Maharashtra. Bookings requested outside of these designated areas will be automatically cancelled.

User Obligations & Booking Accuracy

When scheduling a pickup, users must:

  • ✓ Provide complete and accurate booking information, including physical address and estimated quantity.
  • ✓ Ensure a responsible adult is present at the designated pickup address during the scheduled timeslot.
  • ✓ Present items clearly sorted by broad category (reusable clothes, bedsheets, appliances) to expedite weighing and evaluation.

Item Evaluation & Rejection Policy

Our field team reserves the absolute right to inspect and reject items on the spot. We explicitly decline clothes or materials that are severely soiled, torn, damp, moldy, infested, or otherwise hazardous. Evaluation values are determined on-site according to condition, weight, and general textile reuse demand.

📢 Operations & NGO Policy

Asha Old Clothes Buyer is not an NGO or non-profit trust. We operate as a private enterprise specializing in commercial textile collection, reuse, sorting, and raw-material recycling. Clothing and household goods donations are integrated directly into our commercial processing pipelines and do not qualify for tax deductions or tax-exempt certificates.

Changes to Terms

We reserve the right to modify these Terms and Conditions at any time. Updates will be published on this page with an updated "Last Updated" timestamp. Continued use of our booking channels constitutes acceptance of the modified terms.
