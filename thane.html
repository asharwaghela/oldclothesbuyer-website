❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our authorized Thane pickup service.

Is Rahul Old Clothes Buyer part of Asha Old Clothes Buyer?
Yes. Rahul Old Clothes Buyer is an authorized local pickup partner of Asha Old Clothes Buyer for Thane East & West.
Do you provide pickup in Thane East & West both?
Yes, we cover all areas of Thane East & West with fast authorized pickup coordinators.
What items do you accept?
Clothes, shoes, bedsheets, curtains, handbags, soft toys, and other reusable household textiles.
Is there a minimum quantity?
A quantity of 5–7 kg is ideal, but pickup availability may vary depending on location.
How is the price decided?
Price is based on item condition, category, and weight, with weighing done transparently on-the-spot.
Do you offer UPI payment?
Yes, payment is available via both cash and digital wallet UPI methods.
Can I donate clothes instead of selling?
Yes, donation pickups are completely free and handled responsibly through our network.
How fast can I get pickup?
Same-day or next-day pickup is available for most Thane locations depending on availability.
