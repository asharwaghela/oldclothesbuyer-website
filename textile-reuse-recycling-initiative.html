❓ FAQ

Common Questions About Textile Reuse & Recycling ♻️

Quick answers about our responsible clothing reuse and recycling support services 🌍

1. What happens to collected clothes? 👕
Collected clothes are sorted based on condition. Wearable clothes may be reused through low-cost second-hand clothing networks, while selected items may support textile recycling practices ♻️
2. Are you an NGO or charitable trust? 🤝
No. Asha Old Clothes Buyer operates as an independent clothes collection, reuse, and recycling service ♻️
3. Why is clothing reuse important? 🌍
Responsible clothing reuse helps reduce textile waste, landfill dumping, and environmental pollution while extending the life of wearable garments 🌱
4. Do you collect only wearable clothes? 👕
Yes. We mainly prefer clean and reusable clothes suitable for responsible reuse support ♻️
5. Do you collect damaged clothes? 🚯
No. We mainly collect clean and reusable clothes. Heavily damaged, wet, contaminated, or unusable clothes may not be accepted for pickup ❌
6. Are collected clothes given away for free? 👕
Not always. Depending on condition, collected clothes may be reused, recycled, or redistributed through affordable second-hand clothing networks ♻️
7. Which areas do you currently serve? 📍
Currently, our doorstep pickup collection network operates across Thane, Mumbai, and Navi Mumbai 🚚
8. How can I book a pickup? 📲
You can contact us through WhatsApp or Call Support to schedule a free clothes pickup 📞. Depending on location, quantity, and manpower availability, pickup may be arranged on the same day or next day 🚚
9. Why should I avoid throwing old clothes into waste? 🌱
Many clothes still have reusable life remaining. Responsible reuse and recycling help reduce environmental pollution and unnecessary textile waste ♻️
10. Do you support bulk clothes pickup? 📦
Yes. We gladly support bulk household, society collection drives, and warehouse clothes clearance pickups depending on quantity and location 🚛
