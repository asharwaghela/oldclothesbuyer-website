FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our clothes buying and donating service in Thane.

What’s the best place to sell old clothes?
For Thane residents, the fastest and most reliable option is a premium doorstep buyer like Asha Old Clothes Buyer. We offer instant valuation, avoid complex online listings or shipping delays, and pay you cash immediately after a quick on-the-spot inspection.
How to best sell old clothes?
Sort your items by category, keep them clean and dry, estimate their approximate total weight, and message or call our team to book a convenient home visit slot. We handle all weighing and pricing details right in front of you.
Which areas do you serve in Thane?
We provide prompt home pickup services in Hiranandani Meadows, Hiranandani Estate (Patlipada), Naupada, the Raymond area, Kolshet Road, Manpada, Balkum, Kasarvadavli, Runwal, and nearby neighborhoods across Thane.
Do you buy household items and appliances too?
Yes, we also buy household electronics, usable TVs, ACs, mobile phones, toys, kitchenware, bags, curtains, and bedsheets in good, functional condition. A final pricing quote is supplied during the home inspection visit.
How is the price decided?
Prices are based transparently on item condition, specific clothing categories, current market demand, and the overall measured weight. Our representative details the calculation process before any transaction.
When do I get paid, and how?
You receive your full payout immediately on-the-spot upon completing the inspection and weight measurement, via your choice of cash or instant UPI transfer.
What items are not accepted?
We decline items that are severely torn, permanently soiled, moldy, unsafe, or infested. We also do not accept hazardous chemical waste, medical waste, or unsafe raw electronics.
Is there a minimum quantity?
An estimate of 8 to 10 kg of clothes is ideal to make a doorstep pickup feasible, but we try to remain highly flexible. Share your details with us on WhatsApp to confirm.
Can I donate instead of selling?
Yes, clothes intended for pure charity donation are picked up free of cost and forwarded directly to local NGOs and shelters. A donation acknowledgement note is available upon request.
How should I prepare for pickup?
We recommend placing your sorted items into large disposable bags or cardboard cartons. This guarantees quick weighing and extremely fast, clean handling during our team's home visit.
Can I reschedule or track my pickup?
Absolutely. You will receive direct confirmation on WhatsApp. Feel free to reply to that chat at any moment to reschedule, cancel, or query our arrival timing.
What’s the fastest way to contact you?
Using the direct "Call Now" or "WhatsApp" buttons on our official website oldclothesbuyer.com is by far the fastest way to receive an immediate response.
