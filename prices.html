Pricing FAQ

Got questions about prices and home pickups? Read details below.

What price will I get?
We pay ₹20–₹30 per item for reusable shirts, pants, sarees, or boutique items, and ₹10–₹20 per kg for daily mixed garments, bedsheets, or miscellaneous textiles.
Is pickup free?
Yes, doorstep collection, weight measuring, sorting, and transport are 100% free with absolutely no hidden charges anywhere in Thane.
Minimum quantity?
To make doorstep routing sustainable, a collection of roughly 30 to 40 clothing items (or 8 to 10 kg minimum weight) is ideal for a pickup.
Damaged clothes?
We only accept reusable clothing in clean, sanitary shape. Heavily torn, soiled, permanently stained, or wet items are declined.
