Pre-Moving FAQ

Check answers to general pre-shifting queries below.

What items can I sell?
We buy old reusable garments, clothes, electronic accessories, computers, washing machines, and functional kitchen appliances.
Do you buy electronics?
Yes, we purchase laptops, mobiles, and home sound equipment, but only in proper working condition. Severely broken items are declined.
Minimum quantity?
We generally require a minimum volume of 30 to 40 garments (or 8–10 kg equivalent) to provide a free home doorstep collection.
Will I get instant cash?
Yes, we calculate the complete on-the-spot valuation, explain it clearly to you, and hand over cash or process instant UPI payment before loaded.
